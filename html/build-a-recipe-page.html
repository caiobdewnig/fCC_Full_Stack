Mug Cake Recipe

Ultra fast recipe of a chewy and chocolatey (think of it as a low-budget petit gateau) yeyeyeyeyey

Igredients:

  • 7 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 7 tablespoons of Nescau
  • 7 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 level teaspoon of instant coffe
  • 7 tablespoons of integral milk
  • 7 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 whole egg

Instructions:

  1. Begin by mixing the dry ingredients(the order doesn't matter) in a medium-sized mug that can go in the microwave. After that, add the milk, the oil and the egg and mix until the mixture is homogenuous.
  2. Put the mug in the microwae for 2min. 45sec. (or 165 sec. in total)
  3. After it's done, take it off the microwave and let it cool down a bit.
  4. Enjoy!
