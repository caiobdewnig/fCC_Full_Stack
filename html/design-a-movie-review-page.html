Chico Bento and the "Mahvelous" Guava Tree

In Vila Abobrinha, Chico Bento tries to climb his beloved guava tree to pick the fruit without the landowner knowing. He discovers that his tree is threatened by the construction of a road in the region. Chico gathers his friends Zé Lelé, Rosinha, Zé da Roça, Tábata, Hiro and the entire community to put an end to the project of the family of Genezinho and Dotô Agripino.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (8.7/10)
